SaaS is a way of delivering centrally hosted applications over the Internet-as a service. Instead of installing and maintaining software, you simply access it via the Internet avoiding complex software and hardware management.

Advantages

-  High Adoption
-  Lower initial costs
-  Painless upgrades
-  Seamless integration


SaaS facilitates remote application hosting and delivery, making this a key adavantage of SaaS. SaaS customers have no hardware or software to buy ,install, maintain, or update. An Interent connection is all that is needed.

PaaS is a complete development and deployment environment in the cloud, with resources that enable you to deliver everything from simple cloud-based apps to more sophisticated cloud-enabed applications. PaaS includes infrastructure but also middleware, devleopment tools, business intelligence services and database management systems.

Advantages

-  Less coding time
-  Ability to add development capabilities without adding staff
-  Ability to develop for multiple platforms more easily


PaaS allows you to avoid the expense and complexity of buying and managing software licenses. You manage the applications and services you develop, and the cloud service provider typically manages everything else.

Infrastructure as a service is an instant computing infrastructure, provisioned and managed over the internet. IaaS helps you avoid the expense and complexity of buying and managing your own physical servers. Each resource is offered as a seperate service component.

Advantages:

-  Eliminates capital expense
-  Improves continuity of business
-  Responds quicker to shifts in the business environment
-  Better security
-  Gets new apps to users faster


In IaaS, the cloud computing service provider manages the infrastructure while you purchase, install, configure and manage your own software.

CaaS is a collection of different vendor services that facilitate business communications. Organisations may use these to lower costs. CaaS is part of SaaS. The core concept of CaaS is that accessing these services is extremely convenient.

Advantages:

-  Lowers costs
-  Increases efficiency
-  Saves a lot of time


Types of CaaS include Voice over Internet Protocol (VOIP) and video conferencing services. The convenience and utility of CaaS and similiar services are rapidly expanding the business world.
